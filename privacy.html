Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary: League Match Companion does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All processing happens locally on your computer.

Introduction

League Match Companion ("the App", "we", "our") is a desktop overlay application for League of Legends. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your Riot ID, summoner name, or account information
  • We do not collect your match history or gameplay statistics
  • We do not track your usage of the App
  • We do not use cookies or analytics
  • We do not collect your IP address or device information

How the App Works

When you use League Match Companion:

  • Match data is fetched directly from the official Riot Games API to your local computer
  • All processing happens locally on your machine
  • Data is not stored after you close the application (except for local cache files to improve performance)
  • No data is transmitted to any external servers owned or operated by us

Third-Party Services

The App interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Riot Games API: Used to fetch match data. Subject to Riot Games' Privacy Notice
  • Overwolf SDK: The overlay platform used to display information in-game. Subject to Overwolf's Privacy Policy
  • Data Dragon CDN: Used to fetch static game assets (champion images, item icons). This is a public CDN operated by Riot Games.

Local Data Storage

The App may store the following data locally on your computer:

  • Cache files: Static game data (champion names, item information) is cached locally to reduce API calls and improve performance. This data contains no personal information.
  • Session logs: Temporary log files may be created during gameplay for debugging purposes. These are stored locally and can be deleted at any time.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit your data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our services. The Riot Games API key used by the App is secured server-side and never exposed to the client application.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
